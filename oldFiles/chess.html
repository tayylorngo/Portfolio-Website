Chess

Chess is a game that I enjoy playing a lot. During high school, I played for my school's team who were once national champions and are one of the best teams in New York state. It was an amazing experience and I had lots of fun competing across the country. While I'm nowhere near being a chess master, I had picked up many achievements playing competitively. At the end of the day, chess is a hobby that I'm proud of and something I enjoy doing.
